Lumos

Story Visualization Reimagined

Lumos revolutionizes storytelling by blending advanced AI with intuitive design. Our platform transforms text into dynamic visual narratives, enabling creators to bring their imagination to life through intelligent scene generation and interactive editing.

AI Architecture

Powered by Stable Diffusion with ControlNet and IP Adapter for precise image generation, combined with our custom NLP pipeline that analyzes story structure and semantic meaning.

Interactive Elements

Our proprietary animation system allows real-time manipulation of generated elements, with physics-based interactions and dynamic lighting powered by Three.js WebGL rendering.
